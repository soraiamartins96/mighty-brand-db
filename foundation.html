01Colours

Main = brand-locked, always together. Product-line groups are situational — pick one per SKU, never mix product palettes within a block.

02Fonts

Two fonts. Montserrat is the workhorse (all headings + body). Flood is the brushy accent — only ever inside a Montserrat headline, used for 1–3 emphasis words, typically in Spark yellow. Headlines run upper-case with kerning 0; body runs +50 kerning with generous leading.

03Buttons

Two sizes × three colours. All carry the offset shadow (-6/6). Spark sits on Jet — its shadow flips to Jet.
